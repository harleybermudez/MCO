Site Sections Guide

The Home Page is your central dashboard.
  • Hero Section: Quick introduction to the project.
  • Dance Carousel: Links to specific genre pages like Hip Hop and Ballroom.
  • Featurettes: Highlights of key sections like "Dance Fundamentals".
Tip: Click the "World of DANCE" logo at the top left of any page to return here instantly.

The Dances tab takes you to our genre library. From there, you can explore specific styles:
  • Folk Dance: History of Philippine and global folk dances.
  • Hip Hop: The 4 pillars of Hip Hop culture (DJing, MCing, Breaking, Graffiti).
  • Contemporary: Techniques from Graham to Cunningham.
  • Ballroom: Standard and Latin disciplines.

This is the practical learning area. Navigate to Dance Fundamentals to find:
  • Rhythm Videos: Introductory videos on musicality.
  • Interactive Positions: A sticky video player on the left that demonstrates the 5 Fundamental Positions of Arms and Feet, with step-by-step text on the right.

Check the Trends Page to see what is currently happening in the dance world.
  • Viral TikTok challenges affecting Folk Dance.
  • Modern competitive trends in Ballroom.
  • New fusion styles in Contemporary.

Video Playback Issues?
  1. Ensure you are using a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari).
  2. Check if the video is muted by default (click the speaker icon).
  3. Videos on the Hip Hop page are set to autoplay when visible; scrolling quickly might pause them.
Missing Images?

If images appear as gray boxes, check your internet connection. We use placeholders if assets fail to load.
